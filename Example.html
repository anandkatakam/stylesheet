Colors

We consciously chose a color system that was outside of the journalism ‘norm’ to serve as an important differentiator - to underscore that we are trying some new things. The colors should generally talk to each other a little bit, giving the feeling of cohesion, thoughtfulness, playfulness, nuance, and boldness (etc). This includes not jsut black, but other colors (different shades of yellow, for example, for slightly different purposes).

NEVER BLACK on anything. Use $default-text-color or $vox-logo-color.

image overlays

Explainers: Image is desaturated and then overlayed with blue automatically (imagemagik) when uploaded to Chorus. The purpose is because the image is generally used as a background image to the card stack OR as a unique-feeling type of content on our homepage differentiated from other normal articles!

Homepage: We built system to detecet primary color in images, and choose complimentary overlay color which is applied via SCSS. The purpose is to give variety on the HP experience and also to make the foregrounded headlines more legible. For regular images, the opacity is 5%. For Beats, Groups, and Streams (items with more text) the opacity is 60%.
